What We Deliver

In-House, Multi-Strategy Investment Management

We develop and manage portfolio strategies directly, not outsourced to third-party models or turnkey platforms. Our Alpha Trend strategy applies tactical, trend-following discipline to pursue growth while managing downside exposure. Our Beta Market strategy provides systematic, index-based core positioning. Both are coordinated within each client's goals, risk tolerance, and tax situation.

Client-Driven Financial Planning

Clients determine the scope of the planning relationship. As their lives evolve, their standards and services adapt accordingly, organized around a defined planning framework rather than a product checklist.

Concierge Service with Depth

We intentionally limit our client list to deliver a personal, anticipatory advisory experience. As a virtual-first firm, we work with clients wherever they are. Interactions, virtual or in person, are attentive and focused on understanding client priorities, not just portfolios.

How We Work

As a fiduciary, HFI is legally obligated to act in the best interest of its clients at all times. The firm is fee-only. We do not sell financial products, earn commissions, or accept compensation from third parties. Our only source of revenue is the fees paid by our clients.

Client assets are held at Charles Schwab, a qualified custodian. Additional qualified custodian relationships are available and can be evaluated at a prospective client's request.
