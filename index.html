About the Firm

Howard Fiduciary Investments LLC is based in Rockford, Michigan. As a fiduciary, the firm is legally obligated to act in the best interest of its clients at all times.

HFI does not sell financial products, earn commissions, or accept compensation from third parties. The firm's only source of revenue is the fees paid by its clients.

Services

Investment Management. Directly managed, ETF-based portfolios custodied at Charles Schwab.

Financial Planning. Retirement income planning, tax coordination, estate review, and risk management, integrated with the investment strategy.
